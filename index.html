Our Philosophy

🌱 Soil Drives Everything

Founded by Tarang Karpe, our farm is built on the belief that healthy soil is the foundation of every thriving ecosystem. We strengthen microbial activity and improve water efficiency to create a self-supporting farm.

🦠

Microbial Activity

We cultivate a living soil ecosystem. Rich microbial communities enhance nutrient cycling and root health naturally.

💧

Water Efficiency

Smart irrigation and soil moisture management ensure every drop counts, reducing waste while maximizing yield.

🔄

Self-Supporting

Rather than isolated production units, every element on the farm supports the others — a true circular ecosystem.

🌿

Organic First

Chemical-free farming practices protect soil biodiversity and produce clean, healthy crops for the market.

Layered Farming

🌳 Multi-Layer Ecosystem

By growing diverse crops across multiple vertical and temporal layers, Tarang's Farm ensures both long-term stability and weekly liquidity.

🌴

Canopy Layer — Tall Trees

Mango, Jackfruit — Long-term backbone crops

🌿

Mid-Canopy Layer

Moringa, Guava, Apple Ber — Medium-cycle yields

🍌

Understory Layer

Banana, Papaya — Fast-growing, regular harvests

🍋

Anchor Orchard — Lemon

475+ trees providing the farm's stable foundation

🌻

Ground Layer — Floriculture

Marigold, Zinnia, Gerbera — Weekly cash flow

🪱

Vermicompost Engine

Working quietly in the background, our biological soil engine enhances root health, boosts microbial activity, and contributes significantly to the overall productivity of every layer.
